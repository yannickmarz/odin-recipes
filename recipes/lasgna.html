Lasagna

My Favorite Lasagna Recipe

For my this tasteful lasagna you will need the following:

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic

How to prepare the Lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook your noodles.
  3. Prepare the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil
  6. Put in the oven
