Tomato Sauce

My very best tomato sauce

For my this tasteful tomato sauce you will need the following:

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Fresh tomatoes

How to prepare the Sauce

  1. Cook the onions in olive oil.
  2. Add tomato paste.
  3. add the fresh tomatoes.
  4. season to your taste
